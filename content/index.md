## Style Qs, real quick answers

Fresh tips for your everyday look.

+------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| **Accordion-Faq**                                                                                                                               |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Sneakers for night—yes or no?                                          | Absolutely! Pair clean kicks with dark jeans or chinos for an easy,   |
|                                                                        | laid-back vibe that still looks sharp.                                 |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| What's hot for beach days?                                             | Bold colors, lightweight fabrics, and don't forget the shades. Keep   |
|                                                                        | it breezy and fun!                                                     |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Mixing sporty with casual—can I?                                       | Yes! Athletic pieces like joggers or hoodies work great with jeans or |
|                                                                        | casual tees. Just balance the look.                                    |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| How do basics get a glow-up?                                           | Add bold accessories, layer smart, or try unexpected color combos.    |
|                                                                        | Small changes, big impact!                                             |
